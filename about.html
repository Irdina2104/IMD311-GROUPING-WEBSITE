ABOUT AROMA COLLECTIONS

AROMA collection is a business that sells 100% handmade candles and is made with full passion. AROMA ensures that customers who buy their products can feel the comfort of the scent. AROMA candles are made using refined fragrance oils that consist of natural ingredients. AROMA also ensures that the fragrances used are chemical-free and environmentally friendly. The owners of AROMA collections are Nurirdina Amani Binti Azizan, A'lyaa Maisarah Binti Khairul Fadzle, Nur Aishah Binti Zainal Abidin and Nurliyana Binti Adnan.
